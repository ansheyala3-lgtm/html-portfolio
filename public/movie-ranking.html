MY FAVOURITE MOVIES OF ALL TIME!

Here are some of my favourite movies that i absolutely love and enjoy to watch!

BANGLORE DAYS

Banglore days is a 2014 Indian Malayalam-language coming-of-age romantic comedy-drama film written and directed by Anjali Menon. The film features an ensemble cast of Dulquer Salmaan, Nivin Pauly, Fahadh Faasil, Nazriya Nazim, Parvathy Thiruvothu and Isha Talwar. The film's soundtrack and score were composed by Gopi Sunder.

FAVOURITE SONGS :

- Ente Kannil Ninakkayi
- Ethu Kari Ravilum

DHURANDHAR

Dhurandhar is a 2025 Indian Hindi-language action film about the indian army. A story that shows us how much the indian army is sacrificing. it has a bit of romance and a hell lot of violence. Ranveer Singh played a very good role in this movie. cant wait for part 2! The film's soundtrack and score were composed by Sachin–Jigar.

FAVOURITE SONGS :

- Gehra Hua
- Run Down The City

RAB NE BANA DI JODI

Rab Ne Bana Di Jodi is a 2008 Indian Hindi-language romantic comedy film directed by Aditya Chopra and produced by Yash Chopra. The film stars Shah Rukh Khan and Anushka Sharma in the lead roles, with Vinay Pathak, Sabyasachi Chakraborty, and Archana Puran Singh in supporting roles. The film's soundtrack and score were composed by Salim–Sulaiman. A very awesome and heart touching movie with heart capturing dialogues and music. even though the story is a bit unrealistic it will make you cry and laugh at the same time.

FAVOURITE SONGS :

- Haule Haule
- Tujh Mein Rab Dikhta Hai
